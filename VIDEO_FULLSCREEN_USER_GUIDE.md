# How to Use Fullscreen Video Feature - Quick Guide

## For Mobile Users (PWA)

### Watching Videos in Fullscreen

#### Method 1: Using the Fullscreen Button
1. Open the Videos page in the Anuranan app
2. Tap on any video thumbnail to open it
3. Look for the **expand icon** (⛶) button at the top-right of the video
4. Tap it to enter fullscreen mode
5. Video will fill your entire screen
6. Tap the **compress icon** (⛶) to exit fullscreen

#### Method 2: Rotate Your Device
1. Open any video
2. Simply **rotate your device to landscape** orientation
3. The video will automatically optimize for landscape viewing
4. Rotate back to portrait to return to normal view

### Important Notes

#### First Time Setup
If you installed the app before this update:
1. **Option A - Soft Reset**:
   - Open the app
   - Pull down to refresh the page
   - The service worker will update automatically
   
2. **Option B - Hard Reset** (Recommended):
   - Remove/uninstall the current PWA app from your home screen
   - Visit the website again
   - Re-add to home screen
   - This ensures the manifest changes take effect

#### Browser Support
- ✅ **Android Chrome**: Full support for fullscreen and rotation
- ✅ **Android Firefox**: Full support for fullscreen and rotation  
- ✅ **iOS Safari**: Rotation works; fullscreen has some limitations but landscape mode is optimized
- ✅ **Samsung Internet**: Full support for fullscreen and rotation
- ✅ **Edge Mobile**: Full support for fullscreen and rotation

### Troubleshooting

#### Video won't go fullscreen
- Make sure you're using the latest version of the app
- Try clearing browser cache and reinstalling the PWA
- On iOS, use landscape rotation instead of fullscreen button

#### Screen won't rotate
- Check that rotation lock is OFF in your device settings
- Android: Swipe down from top → Look for rotation lock icon
- iOS: Swipe down from top-right → Check rotation lock in Control Center

#### Video controls are hidden in fullscreen
- They will appear when you tap on the video
- The fullscreen exit button (⛶) stays visible at the top

### Best Viewing Experience

**For Short Videos (1-3 minutes)**:
- Portrait mode works great
- Use the default modal view

**For Longer Videos or Tutorials**:
- Rotate to landscape or use fullscreen button
- Better view of details and demonstrations
- More comfortable for extended viewing

**For Performances**:
- Landscape mode recommended
- Use fullscreen for immersive experience
- Wider view captures stage presence better

## For Desktop Users

### Fullscreen on Desktop
1. Click on any video thumbnail
2. Click the **fullscreen button** (⛶) in the top-right
3. Video fills your entire screen
4. Press **ESC** key or click the exit button to return

### Keyboard Shortcuts
- **ESC**: Close video or exit fullscreen
- **Click outside video**: Close modal
