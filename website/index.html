Best Bengali Recitation Institute in Kolkata


Nestled in the heart of Kolkata, ANURANAN Recitation Training Institution is a celebration of Bengali culture, literature, and performing arts.

We are dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of Bengali recitation (Abritti), while also nurturing talents in English and Hindi poetry recitation. Our institute serves as a cultural hub where students learn not just the art of recitation, but also develop confidence through anchoring and acting classes.

Our Mission at Anuranan

To inspire and empower students to express themselves through the timeless art of recitation, anchoring, and acting, while keeping the essence of Bengali literature and culture alive for generations to come.

Bengali Cultural Heritage

Drawing inspiration from the timeless legacy of Bengal's greatest literary minds

রবীন্দ্রনাথ ঠাকুর

Rabindranath Tagore

1861-1941

Nobel laureate, poet, composer, and philosopher. His works celebrate humanity, nature, and spirituality. We teach his timeless poems and songs that continue to inspire generations.

"যেখানে দেখিবে ছাই, উড়াইয়া দেখো তাই, পাইলেও পাইতে পারো অমূল্য রতন"

— Rabindranath Tagore

কাজী নজরুল ইসলাম

Kazi Nazrul Islam

1899-1976

The Rebel Poet, musician, and revolutionary. Known for his fiery poems on equality, freedom, and love. His powerful verses ignite passion and courage in every reciter.

"বল বীর, বল উন্নত মম শির!"

— Kazi Nazrul Islam

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I learn Bengali recitation in Kolkata?

ANURANAN Recitation Training Institution is one of the best places in Kolkata to learn Bengali recitation (Abritti), offering classes for all age groups in Paschim Barisha.

What courses does Anuranan institute offer?

We offer comprehensive courses in Bengali Recitation (Abritti), Audio Drama (Shrutinatak), Anchoring, and Acting workshops.

Is there an age limit for joining recitation classes?

No, Anuranan welcomes students from ages 3 to 65+. We believe it's never too late to learn Bengali Kobita and culture.

Does Anuranan provide online recitation classes?

Please contact us directly at +91-8910621825 to inquire about current online class availability for Bengali recitation.
