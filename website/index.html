Nestled in the heart of Kolkata, Anuranan Recitation Training Institute is a celebration of Bengali culture, literature, and performing arts.

We are dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich heritage of Bengali recitation, while also nurturing talents in English and Hindi poetry recitation. Our institute serves as a cultural hub where students learn not just the art of recitation, but also develop confidence through anchoring and acting classes.

Our Mission

To inspire and empower students to express themselves through the timeless art of recitation, anchoring, and acting, while keeping the essence of Bengali literature and culture alive for generations to come.
